About Ideal Fitouts

Delivering exceptional commercial fit-outs across Auckland since 2016.

18+ Years Experience

Founded on Experience, Built on Trust

Ideal Fitouts Limited was founded by Samuel in 2016. With over 18 years experience as a qualified builder, Samuel has the skill set and experience that enables him to manage and deliver high quality Fitout projects.

Samuel monitors and oversees all Ideal Fitouts projects directly to ensure the quality and the workmanship in each project is at a high standard.

We specialise in Interior Aluminium Partitioning for Office, Retail, and Education spaces, based in Auckland, New Zealand.

Execution of the Project is not just about completing the Project but the whole experience. We pride ourselves in having a friendly, respectful and informative approach to our clients. It is important to us to establish a trusting relationship through good communication and simple processes. Your experience is important to us.
Samuel, Director of Ideal Fitouts
